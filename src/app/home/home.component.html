About

Back by popular demand…. DevFest 2019!

DevFest is a 100% community organized, full-day developer conference featuring world-class technology thought leaders and members of the Google product team.

What does that actually mean?

We’re bringing you an all-in one opportunity to up-level your game as a techie, whether you’re a total beginner, an expert, or somewhere in-between…

… to learn about new technologies, with practical strategies to learn and implement them…

… to connect with the LA tech community in a setting where we’ve done the hard work for you…

… the event’s structure and our support team are there for you, ensuring everyone is welcomed and included…

… where we can all get inspired by learning about each other and exploring cutting edge technologies!

Most conferences and networking events leave you in a room to fend for yourself. They focus on the information, and maybe even the implementation…

But they’re completely leaving out the greater part of the equation: human interaction. And we don’t blame them - it’s easy to overlook that the humans are the most important part of building great tech!

Even with the world’s greatest ideas , you can’t transcend your career blocks if you’re disconnected from the community around you…

If you don’t think you’re cool and interesting enough for others to connect with, you won’t put yourself out there.

Trust us, we know you might be thinking… 

I know enough (technologies, people), I just need to know what to do next.

We’ve got you. We’re going to cover it all, and more when you join us at DevFest LA 2019! You’ll go deep with us, and we’ll be with every one of you every step of the way. “When we come together to exchange ideas, amazing things can happen!”

HANG ON A MOMENT, WHO ARE YOU PEOPLE?

We are community organizers leading Google Developer Groups (GDGs) in the Los Angeles area!

You might be thinking... “Ok cool, what in the world is a GDG?”...

... a GDG is a Google-sponsored, 100% community organized group bringing developers and other techies together to explore new technologies and build deep connections...

... Phew, that’s a mouthful!

In practice, all this means is: we’re a bunch of people who meet regularly, support one another, and learn about cool tech stuff. Sometimes, we even facilitate each other getting new jobs! Pretty cool, yeah?

We pinky promise that when you come to DevFest, you will have fun, you will learn something, and you will connect with someone new!

FAQ'S

Who is this event for?

Everyone and anyone interested in tech - from beginner developers to experienced professionals - are welcome to join. Our content is curated to serve everyone from total beginners to leading experts.

What if I don’t know anyone there, will I feel out of place?

We have curated an experience to make sure you are woven into the community whether you come with someone you know or arrive by yourself. You’ll find it easy to connect with other like-minded techies as we’ve designed the whole experience to be fun, educational and inspiring. This is more than just a group of people at an event, it’s a supportive community where you are 100% welcome and accepted, no matter where you’re at.

Is this something I can afford to attend?

Affordability is a priority for us - a full price ticket is less than the cost of your average working lunch in LA, and no one is selling you anything at the event. For those facing financial hardship, please reach out to our team - scholarships are available.

P.S. A little birdy told us your boss will probably sponsor your attendance as part of your learning and development. Did we mention your ticket is the price of a single lunch? ;) 

Can I bring a friend?

Our goal is to reach as many people as possible, and to connect more people in our LA tech community. Please invite your friends to share this powerful experience with you – send this link to them right now!

What is the dress code?

Whatever makes you feel your best is perfect. Anything you feel inspired to wear that makes you feel like you, wear that! 

What happens after I buy my ticket?

You'll receive a confirmation from Eventbrite. There's no need to print your ticket to bring along to the event as we'll have you on the guestlist. Please email us if you'd like to change the name on your ticket, as they're non-refundable.

Is lunch provided?

YES! Brains need fuel to function, so lunch will be provided. For more information regarding our efforts to accommodate dietary needs, please reach out to our team.

Can I claim my ticket as a business expense?

Training and development can often be claimed as a business expense, to write off against tax. We are not authorized to give legal or tax advice, so please verify with your accountant and attorney to understand your expenses. 

Can you really up-level my tech skills in just 1 day?

Yes - there is power in immersion, accelerating your results. Also, our in-person experience will support you in creating meaningful relationships which will help you in cultivating your ongoing success. 

What if I can’t keep up and everyone’s more advanced than me?

There will be people at all different levels and you are 100% welcome no matter where you’re at.
